• Heat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C.)
  • Arrange sliced peaches evenely in an 8" x 8" baking dish.
  • Mix flour, brown sugar, butter, cinnamon, and salt in a bowl until crumbly.
  • Fold oats into flour mixture; sprinkle mixture evenly over peaches, pressing down lightly.
  • Bake in the preheated oven (about 30 minutes) until crispy and golden brown on top.
  • Let cool and enjoy!
